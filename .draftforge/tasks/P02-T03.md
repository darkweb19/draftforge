# P02-T03 — Recorded plan revision flow

Status: done

## Objective

Allow an approved plan to change only through a recorded revision that bumps the
planning revision, preserves answered interview context, and reconciles the new
task graph against work already completed or in flight.

## Acceptance criteria

- A revision requires an explicit reason and actor, and is recorded in the
  planning artifact with its predecessor revision.
- Answered questions carry forward; the new revision may add questions but must
  not silently discard recorded answers.
- Revision does not reset progress: tasks that are `done` stay `done` unless the
  new plan explicitly reopens them, and reopening is recorded.
- Removing a task that is `active` or `done` is rejected unless the revision
  explicitly reopens or retires it with a recorded reason.
- Approval of a revision re-materializes phase, ADR, and task files without
  destroying user edits to files it does not own.
- A revision is not approved implicitly; the P02-T01 approval gate still applies.
- Deterministic fixtures cover revise-before-approval, revise-after-approval, and
  a rejected destructive revision.
- `npm run check` passes.
